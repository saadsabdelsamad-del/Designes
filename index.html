Our Story

Built on Expertise.
Proven Over Decades.

Designes was built from nothing. In 1995, its founder started the company with a simple conviction: that quality engineering, delivered honestly, would always find its clients. What followed was not a smooth ascent — it was three decades of perseverance through conditions that broke far larger firms. Wars, economic collapse, currency crises, political paralysis — Lebanon has endured them all, and so has Designes. While competitors closed their doors and others left the country, he stayed. He kept his team together, honored his commitments, and continued delivering work that met the highest technical standards, regardless of the circumstances outside. Today, Designes stands as a testament to what one determined man can build — and sustain — through sheer integrity and refusal to quit. From hospitals and national bank branches to luxury residences and commercial towers, every project in our portfolio carries the same weight: the word of someone who has never walked away from a promise.
