Our Story

Built on Expertise.
Proven Over Decades.

Designes was founded in 1995 with a clear mission: to deliver engineering solutions that meet the highest technical standards across Lebanon. What began as a specialist mechanical engineering firm has grown into a fully integrated MEP contracting company trusted by hospitals, banks, real estate developers, and government entities.

Over three decades, we have built our reputation project by project — never compromising on quality, timeline, or safety. Our team brings deep expertise across HVAC, plumbing, firefighting systems, and electrical works, ensuring seamless coordination across all building services.

From the complete mechanical scope of Ein Wazein Hospital to multiple Banque du Liban branches, our portfolio reflects the breadth of sectors that trust Designes to deliver.

What We Do

End-to-End MEP Services

We handle every mechanical, electrical, and plumbing discipline in-house — delivering fully coordinated building systems from design through commissioning.

❄️

HVAC Systems

Complete heating, ventilation, and air-conditioning solutions engineered for comfort, energy efficiency, and code compliance in any building type.

  • Central & split AC systems
  • Chiller plant design & installation
  • Fresh air & ventilation systems
  • Cleanroom & critical environment HVAC
  • BMS integration & controls
🔧

Plumbing & Drainage

Full plumbing packages covering water supply, drainage, and sanitary systems — designed for reliability and long-term performance.

  • Domestic cold & hot water systems
  • Drainage & sewage systems
  • Pumping stations & storage tanks
  • Roof drainage & rainwater systems
  • Pressure-reducing & booster systems
🚒

Firefighting Systems

Life-safety fire suppression and detection systems engineered and installed to meet international codes and local civil defense requirements.

  • Wet & dry sprinkler systems
  • Fire hose reel networks
  • FM200 & gaseous suppression
  • Fire detection & alarm systems
  • Smoke extraction & pressurization
⚡

Electrical Works

Comprehensive electrical contracting — from low-voltage distribution networks to advanced building automation and intelligent systems.

  • LV power distribution & cabling
  • Lighting design & installation
  • Earthing, bonding & lightning protection
  • Generator & UPS systems
  • Data, voice & structured cabling
